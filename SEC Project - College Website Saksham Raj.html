    Faculty

    A faculty is a group of employees at a college or university who teach students. They are also known as academic staff.

    Faculty members may include: Teachers, Lecturers, Researchers, Scholars, Professors. Faculty members specialize in different fields of study and teach various subjects.

    They may be organized into departments, such as:

    1. Faculty of Science
    2. Faculty of Engineering
    3. Faculty of Arts
    4. Faculty of Commerce
    Faculty members may also be organized into divisions within a college or university. These divisions may be called: Colleges, Schools, Academic departments.
